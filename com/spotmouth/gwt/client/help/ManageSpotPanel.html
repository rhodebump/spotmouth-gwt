Lock

Locking a spot prevents the modification of the spot by unauthorized people. A spot is automatically locked whenever the first party edits the spot. If a second party attempts to edit the spot, they will not be authorized to do so, however, an email will be sent to the first party when a link to grant access to the second party.

In the case that the first party is not a valid owner of the spot, the valid owner of the stop will have to contact spotmouth using the contact form to obtain access to the locked spot.

Only when a spot is locked can coupons, events, websites, mailing lists and a host of other features can be utilized for the spot.
