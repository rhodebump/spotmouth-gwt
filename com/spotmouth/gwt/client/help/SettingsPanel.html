Settings Panel

The settings panel allows you to manage certain things with the spotmouth application.

If you choose to remember your login/password when you signin, the username/password will be stored. When it is stored, it will be displayed here.

Save settings will save what is displayed for your username/password. Please note that this is not how to change your password.

Clean settings will delete the username and password and any other settings that are stored as part of spotmouth.
