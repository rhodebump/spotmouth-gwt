Search Results

Sorting by username will sort the results by the user who last posted.

Sorting by total ratings will sort the result by the total number of times a spot has been rated (using the best or worse) options.

Sorting by average ratings will sort by total sum of ratings divided by number of total ratings.

Sorting by mark count will sort by the total number of marks made to the spot.

Sorting by distance will sort the results in ascending distance from your location. Things closer will appear at the top of the list, things farther will appear at the bottom of the list.

Remove sorts, this will remove all the sorting.
